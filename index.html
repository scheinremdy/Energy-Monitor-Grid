About This System

An Energy Monitor Grid Design is a system used to track, manage, and optimize energy consumption within a specific area or grid. The design incorporates various technologies, such as smart meters, sensors, and data analytics tools, to monitor electricity use, identify inefficiencies, and provide real-time data. This system can be applied to a wide range of areas, including residential, industrial, or public grids, and plays a crucial role in modernizing energy infrastructure.
