About This System

The Energy Monitor Grid is designed to track, manage, and optimize energy consumption within specific regions. By incorporating technologies such as smart meters, sensors, and data analytics tools, it monitors electricity usage, identifies inefficiencies, and provides real-time data. This system is applicable to various sectors, including residential, industrial, and public grids, playing a crucial role in modernizing energy infrastructure.
